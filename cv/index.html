About me

2010: How it all started

At the age of 1.5 years my passion begun.

I saw my parents and my big sister skiing and snowboarding and it was clear for me that I want to do this as well.
So I was looking for the old skis from my sister, put them and walked up (or better crawled up) the mountain.

One season later I already got my first carving skis and my helmet. The real fun started ;-)

2016: Am I a Freeskier?

Yes, I started very early with jumping all the big kickers in Hochybrig (not BigMama).

No, in my heart I was always a snowboarder. But my father only allowed me to snowboard after I had done my first flip with the skis. So I was practice and practice till I got it..

2017: Yes I got it!

Now that I'm a skiing pro ;-), I'm finally allowed to snowboard. Sure I started right like on my skis and with the biggest kicker I had done before with the skis. (see the video)

It didn't take long and my snowboard skills were better than I had ever been with my skis.

Now I'm always in the parks and practice and practice ....

2018: BASE discovered my talent

My practice was not without success.

The BASE talent scouts saw me in the park and wanted me to join the team.

Of course I couldn't say no...

2019: My first successes - as a BASE athlete

After the hard summer training at BASE I was a little nervous how the first contest season would go.

But the hard training pays off and the first good results follow. --> On the picture Laax Open

I am getting better and better in the halfpipe and in the slopestyle and so I can also go to the Swiss Championships with good cheer.
